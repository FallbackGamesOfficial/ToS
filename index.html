Room Activity Logging

To maintain a safe and fair gaming environment, we log certain player activity, including:

  • PlayFabID (a unique identifier for players)
  • Photon Nickname (the name used in-game)
  • Room Code (the identifier of the room a player has joined)

This information is collected for the purpose of moderation and enforcement of our community guidelines, including investigating reports of inappropriate behavior, cheating, or rule violations.

How We Use This Information

The logged information may be used by authorized moderators to review player activity and take necessary action, such as warnings, temporary bans, or permanent bans.

All logs are stored in a secure environment and are only accessible to authorized personnel. We do not share this data with third parties except when required by law.

Your Privacy

We value your privacy and only collect the information necessary to ensure the integrity of the game and its community.

By using our service, you consent to the collection and use of this information for the purposes outlined above.

If you have any questions or concerns about how your data is handled, please contact us at FallbackGamesSupport@protonmail.com.
